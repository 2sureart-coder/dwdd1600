Welcome to Music Monday

What We’re About

Music Monday is a space built for people who want to discover intentional, community-driven indie music. We spotlight new artists, niche subgenres, and the stories behind the sounds that shape the modern indie scene.

The indie world is full of raw creativity, DIY production, and supportive artists who uplift each other. Our goal is to help you explore music that feels personal, thoughtful, and emotionally rich.

Where to Start

If you’re new to indie music, here are a few ways to begin your journey:

  • Try a new genre you’ve never heard before
  • Look up interviews from artists you admire
  • Join online forums and indie playlists

How We Curate

Every Monday, we choose music based on three factors:

  1. Artist intention and storytelling
  2. Community engagement and collaboration
  3. Innovation in sound & production
